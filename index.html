How to submit an Issue

Please include the following information to help us address your feedback efficiently:

  • Issue Description: Briefly explain the problem you are facing.
  • Steps to Reproduce: Describe the actions you took before encountering the issue.
  • Device Information: Your device model and operating system version (e.g., iPhone 13, iOS 17.0).
  • Screenshots or Screen Recordings: If possible, provide images or videos illustrating the problem.
  • Additional Notes: Any other relevant information.
